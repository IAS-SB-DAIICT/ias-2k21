About Synocon’20

IEEE DAIICT Student Branch

Have you ever wondered about what all goes into high profile paper presentation conferences? Are you someone who loves to convert their brilliant ideas into technical reality or just enjoys learning about the new projects around? Then you’re at the right place!

IEEE IAS DAIICT Student Branch Chapter in collaboration with WIE Affinity Group DAIICT proudly presents Synocon 2020; a highly extensive and informative Project and Poster presentation event on 21st and 22nd March 2020 .

The 2-day event will walk you through some amazing workshops and talks along with an interaction and competition with like-minded people. So put your brains at work and come up with some phenomenal and path-breaking projects as we have a total prizes worth 75K to give away.
